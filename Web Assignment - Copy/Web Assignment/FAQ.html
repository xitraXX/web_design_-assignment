E-Gift

Easy peasy! If you’re looking to send someone an e-Gift, just choose which e-Gift you’d like to gift and proceed to complete the details and your order. The recipient will receive an email with the e-Gift, and you’ll receive the order confirmation email. The e-Gift can only be redeemed on our online store www.insidescoop.com.my for all products.

They come in denominations of RM20, RM50, and RM100.

The e-Gift can be used for any items on our online store, subject to availability of items upon order. If the item price exceeds the amount of the e-Gift, the e-Gift holder will need to pay for the balance. If the item price is less than the amount of the e-Gift, we will not reimburse the unused value.

The e-Gift is valid for 6 months. The expiration date is stated on the e-Gift before you make the purchase!
